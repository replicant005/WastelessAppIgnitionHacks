FAQs

Is it safe?

Keep items chilled as required and share accurate expiry info. Only post food you would eat yourself.

What can I share?

Packaged goods, baked items, produce, cooked meals (clearly labeled), and more—follow local guidelines.

Where is it available?

WasteLess works anywhere—list items with your area and arrange pickup via chat.

Is it really free?

Yep. Sharing food is free. Please be kind and respectful when coordinating pickups.
